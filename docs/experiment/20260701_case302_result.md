## Conclusion

The high-ratio idea is blocked by ONNX type/opset constraints:

- opset 9 keeps the compact attribute-form Slice but does not allow bool Pad in
  this runtime;
- opset 10/11 may allow bool Pad, but force Slice conversion that costs more
  params than the removed float16 intermediate.

No ONNX changed and no submit was made.

## Next Step

Continue looking for low-cost high-ratio candidates, but require the replacement
to keep existing compact opset forms or remove enough memory to pay for any
opset/schema conversion.  In small graphs, a handful of new Slice/Pad
initializers can erase the entire expected gain.
